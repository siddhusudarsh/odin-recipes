Butter chicken

Description

This sweet coleslaw recipe tastes like the slaw served at popular fried chicken or fish restaurants. It's excellent with burgers or on top of BBQ pork sandwiches, too!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the coleslaw mix, mayonnaise, sugar, and milk. Stir until well combined.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
  3. Serve chilled as a side dish or on top of sandwiches.
  4. Enjoy your delicious sweet coleslaw!
